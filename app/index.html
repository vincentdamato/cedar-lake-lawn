A price sized from the public record, not a windshield guess

One fixed rate per cut, with the tax shown

The same per-square-foot rate applies to every lawn, posted before you book, with Wisconsin's service tax itemized beside it. No packages, no prepayment, no upsell to learn what you'll pay. You're billed per visit, after the work is done.

No contract, billed per visit

Owner-operated, the same crew on a set route

Good to know

Questions, answered.

What's included in a cut?

Every visit is a mow, a trim along the edges, and a full blow-off of your walks and drive. Fresh Edge adds a crisp mechanical edge; Premier adds tidy bed lines and detail work. Pick your level right in the quote tool.

Is there a contract?

No. We earn next week's cut by showing up for this one. Skip a week, pause for vacation, or stop anytime — nothing to sign and nothing to cancel.

How does billing work?

Per cut, after the work — no packages, no prepay. You get a simple total plus Wisconsin's service tax, and you pay by whatever's easiest for you.

How accurate is the quoted price?

It's an estimate, sized from your lot on county parcel maps rather than guessed over the phone. We confirm it in person before the first cut — especially on larger properties, where we check the actual mowable area with you.

What if it rains?

We watch the forecast and shift your cut a day or two when we have to. You'll hear from us before we come — never a surprise.

Do you do more than mowing?

Mowing is where we start. Edging, beds, and seasonal cleanups are rolling out at the same measured pricing. Tell us what your property needs.
